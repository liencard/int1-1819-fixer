Nothing is worse then having weird weather when you go flying, so we specialised in fixing different weather situations that might occur. You will always have good weather conditions when you take your air balloon out for a flight.

In case of bad weather, the fixer will perform a sun dance thereby you will have perfect flying weather.

Soft landing

Landings are known to be rocky. The fixer will attend for a soft landing by preparing a bouncy castle as landing spot. The air balloon will not directly touch the ground. It will hover a couple of meters above ground. The bouncy castle will be placed directly below, ready for you to jump on to.

Price/day

€49,00
